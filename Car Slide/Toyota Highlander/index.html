Overview

Toyota Highlander is a mid-sized family SUV that combines comfort, performance and efficiency in one complete package. With a modern and aerodynamic exterior design, the Highlander looks elegant but remains tough. Powered by a powerful but fuel-efficient petrol or hybrid engine, this car offers a choice of Front-Wheel Drive (FWD) or All-Wheel Drive (AWD) to support performance in various road conditions. The interior is designed for maximum comfort with a capacity of up to 7-8 passengers, equipped with premium materials, advanced technology features such as a large touch screen with Apple CarPlay and Android Auto connectivity, as well as various advanced Toyota Safety Sense security systems. Highlander is the ideal choice for families looking for an SUV that is reliable, comfortable and equipped with the latest technology.
