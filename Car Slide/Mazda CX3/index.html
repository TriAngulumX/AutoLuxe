Overview

The Mazda CX-3 is a compact SUV that offers a blend of elegant design, powerful performance and modern features. With its compact dimensions, the CX-3 is perfect for maneuvering in urban areas. Its distinctive Mazda exterior design gives it a premium and sporty feel. Inside the cabin, the CX-3 presents a comfortable and driver-oriented interior. Equipped with the efficient Skyactiv engine, the CX-3 provides a pleasant driving experience. In addition, the CX-3 is also equipped with various advanced safety features to provide maximum protection for drivers and passengers.
