HURACÁN EVO

The Lamborghini Huracán EVO is an iconic supercar with an aggressive aerodynamic design, luxurious interior, and advanced technology. Powered by a 5.2-liter V10 engine producing 640 HP, it accelerates from 0 to 100 km/h in just 2.9 seconds, with a top speed of 325 km/h. Smart technologies like LDVI (Lamborghini Dinamica Veicolo Integrata) and all-wheel drive (AWD) ensure optimal performance in various conditions.

SOUND ENGINE

Feel the engine of Aventador SVJ. Get ready to experience unprecedented driving emotions.

INTERIOR

The interior of the Lamborghini Huracán EVO is designed to deliver a luxurious yet sporty driving experience. Its cabin showcases a perfect blend of premium materials, such as Alcantara leather and carbon fiber, combined with cutting-edge technology. The center console is equipped with an intuitive 8.4-inch touchscreen, allowing the driver to control navigation and vehicle settings with ease.

EXTERIOR

The exterior of the Lamborghini Huracán EVO reflects an aggressive and futuristic aerodynamic design, crafted to maximize both performance and aesthetics. The front features sharp lines with large air intakes that enhance airflow and create a bold appearance.
