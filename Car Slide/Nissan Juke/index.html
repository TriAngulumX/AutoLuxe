Overview

Nissan Juke is a compact crossover SUV that combines bold design, agile performance, and innovative technology. Known for its distinctive, eye-catching styling, the Juke features unique round headlights, sharp body lines, and a high-riding stance that sets it apart from other vehicles in its segment. Despite its compact size, the Juke offers a surprisingly spacious and comfortable interior, with advanced tech features like a touchscreen infotainment system, smartphone connectivity, and safety features such as automatic emergency braking and lane-keeping assist. Powered by efficient turbocharged engines, the Juke provides a fun and responsive driving experience, making it an ideal choice for urban drivers who seek a stylish, practical, and versatile vehicle for daily commutes or weekend adventures.

Technical Specification

  • Engine Type
    1.0-liter 3-cylinder turbocharged petrol engine
  • Transmission
    6-speed manual or 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission
  • Drivetrain
    Front-wheel drive (FWD) or all-wheel drive (AWD) in certain models
  • Parking Assistance
    Rear-view camera and parking sensors
  • Climate Control
    Air conditioning or dual-zone climate control depending on the trim
