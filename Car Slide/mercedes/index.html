Overview

The Mercedes-Benz CLA-Class 200 is one of the hatchback models that offers a perfect blend of elegant design, dynamic performance and advanced technology. It is equipped with an efficient yet powerful turbocharged engine, providing a responsive and smooth driving experience. The interior is designed with high-quality materials and modern features, such as the MBUX infotainment system with a large touchscreen, smartphone connectivity, and innovative driver assistance technologies. The exterior exudes a sporty and aerodynamic aura with distinctive sharp lines, while practicality and comfort remain top priorities. The CLA 200 is the ideal choice for those seeking style, luxury and performance in one package..
