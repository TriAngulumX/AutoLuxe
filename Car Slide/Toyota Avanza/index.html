Overview

Toyota Avanza is one of Toyota's flagship Multi-Purpose Vehicles (MPVs) designed to meet the needs of modern families. Featuring a more elegant and sporty design compared to its predecessor, Toyota Avanza offers a spacious and comfortable cabin, accommodating up to seven passengers. This car is equipped with advanced safety features such as Vehicle Stability Control (VSC), Hill Start Assist (HSA), and ABS + EBD, enhancing driving safety. Additionally, the 2022 Avanza comes with an efficient engine and the latest technology, such as a touchscreen infotainment system compatible with Android Auto and Apple CarPlay, making it an ideal choice for those seeking a versatile, fuel-efficient, and affordable vehicle.
