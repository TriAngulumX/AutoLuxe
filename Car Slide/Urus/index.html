URUS SE

Urus SE is the next step in Automobili Lamborghini’s journey toward complete hybridization. As the first PHEV (Plug-in Hybrid Electric Vehicle) version of the luxury Super SUV, the SE sets a new benchmark for the whole segment in terms of comfort, performance, efficiency, emissions, and driving experience.

INTERIOR

The interior of the Lamborghini Urus SE is the pinnacle of modern luxury designed to provide uncompromising comfort and style. The cabin is adorned with the finest materials, such as high-quality Alcantara leather and subtle carbon fiber accents, creating an elegant and futuristic atmosphere. The ergonomic center console is equipped with state-of-the-art technology, including an intuitive dual touchscreen and a cutting-edge infotainment system. Every detail, from the precise seat stitching pattern to the customizable ambient lighting, reflects Lamborghini's signature design perfection. With ample space and specially designed seats for maximum comfort.

EXTERIOR

The Lamborghini Urus SE features a stunning exterior with a perfect blend of luxury and aggressiveness. Its athletic and aerodynamic silhouette is accentuated by sharp lines and contemporary details, reflecting Lamborghini's iconic design heritage. The signature Y-design LED headlights, combined with the large, dominant grille, give it a bold yet elegant character. Premium paint finishes and carbon fiber accents on the body add to the aura of exclusivity, while large, precision-designed alloy wheels emphasize the Urus SE's dominance on the road. A design masterpiece that combines modern technology with a luxurious aesthetic like no other.
